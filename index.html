    Some mind twister questions:

  • Duckworth? Refers to the Duckworth-Lewis method, challenging users to recall or explain the context.
  • Googly? A type of delivery bowled by a leg-spinner that spins in the opposite direction to a normal leg-spin, surprising the batsman.
  • Maiden? An over in which no runs are scored by the batsman.
  • Yorker? A delivery that is pitched right at the batsman’s feet, making it difficult to hit.
  • Chinaman? A left-arm wrist spinner’s delivery that spins from right to left
